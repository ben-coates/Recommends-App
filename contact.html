FAQ

Do I need to have a Facebook account to use the app?
For now, a Facebook account is required to use Recommends. In the future, other social network will be added. Because of the need to populate a list of people you trust, no exclusive Recommends account features are planned.

If I accidentelly ignore a request is there a way to respond?
All request, even the one's you've ignored can be found in your "feed" under the "friends" tab.

Can I give more than one recommendation/repsonse?
Aboslutely. Just click the give button on any request and you can give as many recommendations as you have.

What happens to request once the time limit expires?
Your friends' request dissapear from your feed once the time limit expires. Any of your request can always be found in your feed under the "me" tab.

Do my request disappear from my feed once they expire?
No, they never disapear that way you can always find old recommendations. They can be found under in your "feed" under the "me" tab.

Can I respond to answers on my request?
All request, even the one's you've ignored can be found in your "feed" under the "friends" tab.

Can I use the app without enabling location services?

You can use the app withouto location services, but you will always have to include a specific location in your requests. You will also need to make sure your Facebook account has an up to date location of residence.
